# Privacy Policy for SinceDays

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Overview

SinceDays ("the App") is a personal milestone tracking app for iOS. 
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

**Short version: We collect nothing. All your data stays on your device.**

---

## Data Collection

SinceDays does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data 
on external servers.

We do not use:
- Analytics tools
- Advertising SDKs
- Crash reporting services
- Any third-party tracking

---

## Data Storage

All data you enter in SinceDays (event titles, dates, notes) is stored 
**locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework.

---

## iCloud Sync

If you are signed in to iCloud, your data may be synced across your 
personal Apple devices via **Apple's private CloudKit database**. 
This sync is:

- End-to-end encrypted by Apple
- Stored in your **personal** iCloud account only
- Not accessible to us in any way

For details, see [Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

---

## Children's Privacy

SinceDays does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including 
children under 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" 
date above. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.
